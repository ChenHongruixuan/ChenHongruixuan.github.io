Research Topic

 My research has always been motivated by how to better monitor, describe and understand changes in our planet's surface, thereby contributing to urban planning, resource management, environmental protection, and sustainable development. Currently, I specifically focus on the following research topics:
  • Multitemporal Remote Sensing Image Processing and Analysis: Change Detection, Damage Assessment, Vehicle Counting
  • Unsupervised Domain Adaptation: Semantic Segmentation
  • Machine Learning: Deep Learning, Unsupervised Learning, Weakly Supervised Learning, Domain Adaptation
