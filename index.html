Short Bio

 I am currently a Ph.D student at Sugiyama-Yokoya-Ishida Lab, the University of Tokyo, advised by Prof. Naoto Yokoya. My research has always been motivated by how to better monitor, describe and understand changes in our planet's surface by studying machine learing and computer vision approaches, thereby contributing to urban planning, resource management, environmental protection, and sustainable development.
